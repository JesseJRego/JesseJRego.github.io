Introduction

My name is Jesse Rego and I’m an aspiring Front End Developer. I’m intrigued by the in’s and out’s of the web and hope to tap into how it all works. I want to create simple but intuitive experiences for users, that are easy to navigate and simple to use. Great design is always the implementation of simple changes with real world uses and removing unusable clutter. There is something really special with simplicity that gives a visual elegance to a website, pair that with smooth load times and responsive pages and you got yourself an amazing product.
